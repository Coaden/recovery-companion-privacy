What is Recovery Companion?

Recovery Companion is a supportive AI-powered mobile app that helps individuals in addiction recovery. Built around core 12-step principles and positive habit-building, it offers thoughtful conversation, mood tracking, secure journaling, and personalized support from AI personalities trained to understand the recovery journey.

Features

  • Daily check-ins with supportive AI Companions
  • Secure journaling and milestone reminders
  • Literature lookup from core 12-step texts
  • Personalized insights based on your journey
  • Always available. Never judgmental.
